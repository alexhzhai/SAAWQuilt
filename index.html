Awareness Artwork Quilt

Presented by Harker Art Club

Partnering with the LIFE board for Substance Abuse Awareness Week, the Harker Art Club and its members have created a quilt of art pieces discussing themes of substance abuse.

With this piece, we strive to raise awareness of the dangers of drug and alcohol abuse and to support victims of substance addiction.
